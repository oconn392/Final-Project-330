Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  1. Q: What is ASUBA?

    A: ASUBA is the African Student Union Black Alliance.

    ASUBA is meant to help spread culturual communications at Morrisville while also being a platform for concerns related to affirmative action on campus.


  2. Q: Where is ASUBA usually hosted at?

    A: We are hosted at Charleton Hall 133, you can find more information from our directions page.


  3. Q: What are the requirements for joining ASUBA?

    A: Generally everyone is allowed to join, but we further detail this at our membership page.


  4. Q: Where can I find information regarding upcoming activities?

    A: We update our activity times and dates constantly at our club activities page!


  5. Q: Is there any important events to keep track of as a member?

    A: Yes! We also try to keep our members informed about any important meetings, activities, or events with our special events page!
